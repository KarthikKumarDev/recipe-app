Spaghetti Recipe

Here's a simple recipe for spaghetti:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add salt and spaghetti to boiling water.
  3. Cook until al dente.
  4. Drain the water.
  5. Top with your favorite sauce and enjoy!
